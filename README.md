# Google Maps Timeline Heatmap

Google recently moved Timeline data from the cloud to on-device storage, removing the ability to view your location history on the web. This app brings that back.

Upload your exported Timeline JSON and see all your visited locations as an interactive heatmap. Frequently visited places glow brighter, making it easy to spot patterns in your movement history.

## Sharing

Share your heatmap with others by copying the URL. The shared link encodes a low-resolution version of your data (~11 km grid) so recipients see the general patterns without your exact locations.

## Privacy

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your location data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.
